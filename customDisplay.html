FAQ

Q1:How do I add an event on the calendar?

A: Click on the day to which you would like to add an event and click on the date or click on new event on the top left hand corner.

Q2:How do I edit an event?

A: Click on the event you want to edit and then click on the edit button located on the bottom of the event box.

Q3:How do I delete an event from the calendar?

A: Click on the event which you would like to delete and click on delete button shaped as a trashcan icon located on left hand corner of the event box.

Q4:How do I change the calendar format?

A: Go to the drop down menu on the right hand top corner and click on it to view four calendar formats: day, 4 days, week, and month.

Q5:How do I get back to today's events?

A: Click on the today button in the left top hand corner to be brought back to today's date and view the events.

Q6:How do I change the months?

A: Click on the arrows provided in the top left hand corner to scroll to previous months and future months.
